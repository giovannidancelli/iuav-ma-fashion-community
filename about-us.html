Iuav Master Fashion Community is a platform designed to connect students, practices and opportunities within the Iuav fashion environment. The project was born from the need to create a shared and accessible space for exchange of knowledge and visibility, encouraging interaction between students, external realities and emerging professional contexts.

Our platform aims to support emerging creative practices through accessibility, collaboration and shared visibility. The project promotes a more participatory approach to fashion education and creative production. Its vision is centered on the construction of a democratic platform where knowledge, resources and opportunities can circulate openly.

The community is conceived as a living system, constantly shaped by the people contributing to it.
