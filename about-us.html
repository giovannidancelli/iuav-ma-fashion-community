IMAC was developed within the New Media and Fashion Communities Studio at IUAV University of Venice. The project emerges from a shared interest in fashion, communication and collective practices within contemporary culture. Students from different backgrounds contribute through research, design and collaborative experimentation. The studio functions as a space where critical thinking and creative production intersect.

IMAC is a platform designed to connect students, practices and opportunities within the IUAV fashion environment. The project was born from the need to create a shared and accessible space for exchange and visibility. Through archives, networks and collaborative tools, IMAC supports the circulation of ideas and creative practices, encouraging interaction between students, external realities and emerging professional contexts.

Our platform aims to support emerging creative practices through accessibility, collaboration and shared visibility. The project promotes a more participatory approach to fashion education and creative production. Its vision is centered on the construction of a democratic platform where knowledge, resources and opportunities can circulate openly.

The community is conceived as a living system, constantly shaped by the people contributing to it.

This is our mission and vision.
