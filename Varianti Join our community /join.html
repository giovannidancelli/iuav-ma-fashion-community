Join the Community

Join the Iuav MA Fashion Community through the dedicated link and actively contribute to the growth of a creative and dynamic network. You will be asked to complete a short form, designed to better understand your profile and aspirations.

You will upload a project that represents you, expressing your vision and approach to fashion. At the same form, you will answer a few questions about your expectations from the Iuav MA Fashion Community and what aspects you believe could be developed or improved.

Once you complete the form, you will be contacted to provide your consent to the publication of your personal data. This step is necessary to make community members visible and foster authentic connections. Your contact information will be used exclusively to keep the network alive and interactive.
