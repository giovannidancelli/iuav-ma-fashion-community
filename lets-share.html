let's share

This page is Iuav Master Fashion Community's blog. Share your projects with the community and show off the results!

How it works:

Find the project/item you're interested in through the Living Archive page, where you can also view the student's profile.

Connect with the student through the platforms and addresses listed in their personal profile.

Obtain the project/item you're interested in through a private agreement.

Share the project/item with the community and comment on the results!
